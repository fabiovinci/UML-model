-- Definition --<br/>The codes of the activities and impacts that occur on and around the site. In the case of the Natura2000 application schema, the values must come from the Natura2000 activity types from Appendix E in the Natura 2000 explanatory notes (Standard Data Form Item 6.1). In the case of the Full application schema, values may also come from Water Framework Directive.<br/><br/>
